About

DECENTRALIZED ECONOMY

TGNT is a utility token designed to reward long term holders. It powers the TGNT ecosystem which is geared towards developing it's unique DEX and NFT marketplace.

  • NFT Marketplace - An NFT marketplace designed to create an easy and safe environment for creators to showcase and sell their works, with great UI for collectors and artlovers to easily navigate and purchase NFTs
  • DEX - A unique decentralized exchange with it's own unique token which will be airdropped to all TGNT holders
  • Passive Rewards - 10% tax is applied to all transactions with 4% being distributed to all TGNT holders
  • Autoliquidity Generation - 4% of the 10% tax is used to provide autoliquidity
  • Quarterly burns - 2% of the 10% tax is dedicated to quarterly burns and donations to charities
